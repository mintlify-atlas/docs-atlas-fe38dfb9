This guide covers how to generate, view, and export various reports from your Mantis project data.

## Overview

Mantis provides comprehensive reporting capabilities to help you analyze project performance, track costs, monitor equipment usage, and generate client-ready documentation.

## Available Reports

### Project Reports

Generate reports at the project level including:
- Project summary and status
- Resource allocation and costs
- Timeline and milestones
- Client contact information

### Equipment Reports

Track equipment across projects:
- Equipment assignment history
- Utilization rates
- Maintenance schedules and completion
- Rental costs and billing
- Retirement tracking

### Work Order Reports

Comprehensive work order analytics:
- Custody chain summaries
- Maintenance activity logs
- Service delivery tracking
- Volume and waste management statistics
- Labor and vehicle utilization

### Financial Reports

Cost tracking and billing:
- Daily rental costs
- Maintenance costs
- Logistics and transport costs
- Project cost summaries
- Billing preparation data

### Shipping Guide Reports

Equipment movement documentation:
- Equipment transfers between locations
- Transport activity logs
- Item manifests
- Cost tracking for logistics

## Generating Reports

### Project Summary Report

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Project">
    Open the project you want to report on from the Projects list.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Access Reports">
    Look for the **Reports** or **Reportes** menu option:
    - May be in the main project navigation
    - Could be in a dropdown menu
    - Might be under a specific tab
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Report Type">
    Choose **Project Summary** or **Resumen de Proyecto** from the available report types.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure Parameters">
    Set report parameters:
    - **Date Range**: Start and end dates for the report period
    - **Include Closed**: Whether to include closed work orders
    - **Detail Level**: Summary or detailed view
  </Step>

  <Step title="Generate Report">
    Click **Generate** or **Generar Reporte** to create the report.
  </Step>

  <Step title="View and Export">
    Once generated:
    - Review the report on screen
    - Export to PDF for printing or sharing
    - Export to Excel for further analysis
    - Save or print directly
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Equipment Utilization Report

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Equipment Reports">
    From the project, navigate to:
    1. **Recursos Asignados** (Assigned Resources) tab
    2. Look for **Reports** or export options
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Report Parameters">
    Configure:
    - **Equipment Type**: Filter by EQUIPO or SERVICIO
    - **Date Range**: Analysis period
    - **Status**: Active, Retired, or All
    - **Include Costs**: Whether to show cost data
  </Step>

  <Step title="Generate Report">
    Click generate to create the utilization report showing:
    - Equipment assignment dates
    - Days in operation
    - Daily costs and totals
    - Maintenance schedule adherence
  </Step>

  <Step title="Export Data">
    Export to:
    - **PDF**: For documentation and client sharing
    - **Excel**: For cost analysis and pivoting
    - **CSV**: For importing into other systems
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Work Order Activity Report

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Work Orders">
    Open the **Planillas de Trabajo** (Work Orders) tab in your project.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Report Type">
    Choose from:
    - **Custody Chain Summary**: All custody chains in period
    - **Maintenance Activity**: All maintenance sheets
    - **Combined Report**: Both custody chains and maintenance
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set Filters">
    Configure filters:
    - **Date Range**: Start and end dates
    - **Status**: DRAFT, CLOSED, or ALL
    - **Technician**: Filter by specific technician
    - **Vehicle**: Filter by specific vehicle
    - **Resource Type**: SERVICIO or EQUIPO
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Metrics">
    Choose what to include:
    - Volume totals (gallons, barrels, cubic meters)
    - Waste type breakdown
    - Service hours and duration
    - Cost summaries
    - Technician and vehicle utilization
  </Step>

  <Step title="Generate and Review">
    Generate the report and review:
    - Summary statistics at the top
    - Detailed line items
    - Charts and visualizations (if available)
    - Cost breakdowns
  </Step>

  <Step title="Export Report">
    Export in your preferred format for distribution or archiving.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Maintenance Calendar Report

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Calendar">
    Click the **Calendario Mantenimientos** (Maintenance Calendar) tab.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Time Period">
    Choose:
    - **Month View**: Current month's scheduled maintenance
    - **Week View**: Current week details
    - **Custom Range**: Specific date range
  </Step>

  <Step title="Filter by Equipment">
    Optionally filter to show:
    - All equipment maintenance
    - Specific equipment only
    - Equipment by type
  </Step>

  <Step title="Generate Calendar Report">
    Create a report showing:
    - Scheduled maintenance dates
    - Completed vs. pending maintenance
    - Upcoming due dates
    - Maintenance frequency compliance
  </Step>

  <Step title="Export Calendar">
    Export as:
    - **PDF**: Printable calendar format
    - **Excel**: Spreadsheet with dates and details
    - **CSV**: For importing into calendar applications
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Export Formats

### PDF Export

**Best for:**
- Client presentations
- Formal documentation
- Archiving
- Printing

**Features:**
- Professionally formatted
- Includes company branding (if configured)
- Read-only format
- Universal compatibility

### Excel Export

**Best for:**
- Financial analysis
- Data manipulation
- Pivot tables and charts
- Integration with accounting systems

**Features:**
- Editable spreadsheet format
- Preserves formulas (where applicable)
- Multiple worksheets for complex reports
- Easy to create custom views

### CSV Export

**Best for:**
- System integration
- Database imports
- Custom analysis tools
- Simple data transfer

**Features:**
- Plain text format
- Universal compatibility
- Lightweight file size
- Easy to parse programmatically

## Common Report Use Cases

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Monthly Client Billing Report">
    **Purpose**: Generate invoice support for monthly billing.

    **Steps**:
    1. Select Work Order Activity Report
    2. Set date range to billing month
    3. Filter status: CLOSED only
    4. Include: Daily costs, maintenance costs, logistics costs
    5. Group by: Service type
    6. Export to Excel for invoice preparation

    **Tip**: Include custody chain details for transparency.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Equipment Utilization Analysis">
    **Purpose**: Analyze equipment ROI and utilization rates.

    **Steps**:
    1. Select Equipment Utilization Report
    2. Set date range: Last quarter or year
    3. Include: All equipment types
    4. Show: Days active, total costs, cost per day
    5. Export to Excel for analysis

    **Tip**: Compare active days vs. total days to identify underutilized equipment.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Compliance Documentation">
    **Purpose**: Generate compliance reports for regulatory requirements.

    **Steps**:
    1. Select Custody Chain Summary Report
    2. Date range: Compliance period
    3. Include: Waste types, volumes, transport details
    4. Group by: Waste type
    5. Export to PDF for submission

    **Tip**: Include all custody chains regardless of status for complete documentation.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Maintenance Schedule Adherence">
    **Purpose**: Verify maintenance is performed on schedule.

    **Steps**:
    1. Select Maintenance Calendar Report
    2. Date range: Review period
    3. Filter: All equipment with maintenance
    4. Show: Scheduled vs. completed dates
    5. Export to Excel for tracking

    **Tip**: Use conditional formatting to highlight overdue maintenance.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Technician Activity Report">
    **Purpose**: Track technician workload and productivity.

    **Steps**:
    1. Select Work Order Activity Report
    2. Group by: Technician
    3. Include: Number of orders, total hours, services performed
    4. Date range: Pay period or month
    5. Export to PDF for review

    **Tip**: Compare across technicians to balance workload.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Report Scheduling

If your Mantis installation supports scheduled reports:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Report Scheduler">
    Navigate to **Reports** > **Scheduled Reports** or similar menu.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create Schedule">
    1. Select the report type
    2. Configure parameters (date range, filters)
    3. Set frequency: Daily, Weekly, Monthly
    4. Choose day/time for generation
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure Distribution">
    Set up delivery:
    - Email addresses for recipients
    - Export format (PDF, Excel)
    - Subject and message template
  </Step>

  <Step title="Activate Schedule">
    Enable the schedule to start automatic generation and delivery.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Monitor Execution">
    Check scheduled report history to verify successful generation and delivery.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Common Issues

### Report Shows No Data
**Problem**: Report generates but shows "No data found" or empty results.

**Solution**:
- Verify date range includes the period with data
- Check filters - may be too restrictive
- Ensure work orders exist and are not all in DRAFT status
- Try removing filters one by one to isolate the issue

### Export Fails
**Problem**: Export to PDF or Excel fails or downloads corrupted file.

**Solution**:
- Check browser popup blocker settings
- Try a different export format
- Reduce date range to limit data volume
- Clear browser cache and retry
- Check for special characters in data that may cause issues

### Incorrect Totals
**Problem**: Report totals don't match expected values.

**Solution**:
- Verify all work orders in period are CLOSED
- Check for duplicate entries
- Ensure costs are entered correctly in source documents
- Review grouping and filter settings
- Compare report total to manual calculation

### Missing Columns
**Problem**: Expected data columns are missing from report.

**Solution**:
- Check column selection settings
- Verify data exists in source documents
- Ensure you have permissions to view sensitive data (costs)
- Try different report templates
